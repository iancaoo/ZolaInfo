About Zola

Zola Zhou is a MSJ 19 candidate at Medill Northwestern University, with a magazine specialization. Being good at math, having squinty eyes, and always eating rice: Zola is none of the above. She is good at conveying stories in a narrative angle and she is always eating noodles (not rice). Rare and endangered species often referred to as a native Chinese studying abroad in the U.S. who manages to be a hinge between the Chinese and the American cultures.

After graduating from the George Washington University with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Communication, Zola has been looking for an answer. Is print media dying or is it reforming to devlope into something new? She previously worked as an associate reporter at Xinhua News Agency Washington DC Bureau, and also interned at Exception Aisa, an advertising company as an account executive. Now Zola is on her Medill journey to seek the answer.
